🎓 About CampusConnectAI

CampusConnectAI is an AI-powered college information assistant developed to help students access important academic and administrative information quickly and efficiently. The platform uses Artificial Intelligence, document processing, and database technologies to provide instant responses to student queries.

The system acts as a smart digital assistant that helps students find information related to admissions, courses, fee structures, placements, internships, scholarships, and campus facilities without having to search through multiple documents or websites.

🚀 Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify access to educational information through intelligent technology. CampusConnectAI aims to improve student experiences by providing fast, reliable, and accessible support whenever information is needed.

🌟 Key Features

  • AI-powered chatbot using Google Gemini AI
  • Smart PDF document search and retrieval
  • Admission and academic information support
  • Fee structure and scholarship guidance
  • Placement and internship information
  • 24/7 access to important college information
  • User-friendly and responsive interface
  • Centralized information management system

💡 Why Choose CampusConnectAI?

Students often spend valuable time searching for information across websites, notices, and documents. CampusConnectAI provides a single platform where users can ask questions naturally and receive accurate answers instantly. This improves accessibility, saves time, and enhances the overall student experience.
